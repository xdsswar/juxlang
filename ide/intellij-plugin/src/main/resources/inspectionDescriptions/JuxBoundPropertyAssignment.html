Reports direct assignments to a property bound one-way via bind(…) (E0973, §P.4.2).

A bound property follows its source; assigning to it directly fights the binding and throws IllegalStateException at runtime in debug builds. The check matches the whole receiver chain (Label.Name, not any Name); an unbind() on the same chain silences it, and bidirectional bindings never report — direct sets are legal there (§P.4.3).
